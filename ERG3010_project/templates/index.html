About our project

Huge, fast-updated music information requires listeners to be able to quickly filter out their favorite music. How can we pick out the favorite songs from thousands of new songs every day?

It is impossible to listen to all of them. Our final project is to build a music website to help listeners find out their favorite songs and singers based on the analysis of lyrics, comments and so on. We extract the features of the songs and show them to the users, making it easier and faster for users to pick out their preferred music.
